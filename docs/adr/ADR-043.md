# ADR-043: Huddle "who's on leave" card

## Status: Accepted (2026-06-30)

## Context

Leaves are plotted per sprint (ADR-038/041), but during the daily huddle the team wants a quick,
at-a-glance answer to "**who's out today, and who's off in the next few days?**" without opening the
Leaves page or reading a calendar.

## Decision

Frontend-only; **no new tool** — reuse `get_all_leaves` / `useAllLeaves`.

- **Pure summary (`src/lib/leaveStatus.ts`).** `summarizeLeaveStatus(allLeaves, { today, horizonDays = 7 })`
  flattens every sprint's leaves into `(assignee, date, type)`, dedupes by `(assignee, date)` (an
  overlapping date counts once), and splits into `today` (date === today) and `upcoming` (1…horizon days
  out, sorted by days away). Past and far-out dates are dropped.
- **Card (`src/components/LeaveStatusCard.tsx`).** On the Dashboard/Huddle sidebar: an "Out today" list
  and an "Upcoming (next 7 days)" list, each row showing the person, the date / "in N days", and a
  leave-type chip (VL / EL / Holiday / Offset). Friendly empty states ("Everyone's in today.", "No
  upcoming leave.").

## Consequences

- The huddle gets the leave picture for today + the coming week from data the team already plots, with no
  backend change and one whole-store read (shared with the Leaves planner's hook pattern).
- The card is board-agnostic because the leaves store is keyed by sprint only — it shows the team's
  plotted leaves regardless of which board is selected. (Acceptable for a single-team POC.)

## Alternatives considered

- **Derive from only the current sprint's leaves.** Rejected — leave that falls just past the sprint
  boundary (next sprint) would be missed; the whole-store scan with a date window is simpler and complete.
- **A new `get_upcoming_leaves` tool.** Rejected — `get_all_leaves` + a pure summary already cover it; no
  need for new surface.
